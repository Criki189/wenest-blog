# Topic blacklist

Topics we tried (or considered and rejected) that don't fit Wenest. Check here before adding new topics to the editorial calendar.

## How to use

When you decide a topic is **not** for Wenest — either after writing it, after research, or after a stakeholder discussion — log it here with the reason.

## Hard rules (do not bother, ever)

- Pure wellness content (skincare, ice baths, hair trends, fitness routines) — off-brand. We do home, not body.
- Direct competitor comparisons by name (TaskRabbit vs Wenest, hipages vs Wenest) — legal exposure, low-quality SEO play.
- Politically charged content (renters' rights debates, housing policy opinions, council politics).
- Specific medical advice (mould and asthma → OK with disclaimers; "cure your child's asthma with this cleaning routine" → never).
- Reviews of named tradies — defamation risk.
